What are Cookies:

1.1 Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, smartphone) when you visit websites. They help us recognize your device and gather information about your interactions with our Platform.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • 2.1 Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the basic functioning of our Platform and enable you to navigate and use its features.
  • 2.2 Analytical and Performance Cookies: These cookies allow us to analyze how users interact with our Platform, track user preferences, and improve its performance and usability.
  • 2.3 Functionality Cookies: These cookies enhance your experience on our Platform by remembering your preferences and choices (such as language or region).
  • 2.4 Advertising and Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements to you and track the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns.

Third-Party Cookies:

3.1 We may also allow third-party service providers to place cookies on your device. These providers may use cookies for various purposes, including analytics, advertising, and social media integration. Third-party cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of these providers.

Your Cookie Choices:

  • 4.1 You can manage or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our Platform.
  • 4.2 Many browsers offer a "Do Not Track" feature that signals websites not to track your online activities. Our Platform currently does not respond to such signals.

Cookie Consent:

5.1 By continuing to use our Platform, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this Policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings.
