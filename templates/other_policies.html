Dispute Resolution Policy

Effective Date: 02.10.2023

Good Faith Efforts:

We are committed to providing a positive experience for our users. If you have a dispute or concern, we encourage you to first contact our support team at support@hindo.in. We will make every effort to address your concerns in a timely and fair manner.

Negotiation and Mediation:

If a dispute arises, both parties agree to negotiate in good faith to reach an amicable resolution. If direct negotiation is not successful, both parties may agree to pursue mediation to resolve the dispute. Mediation involves a neutral third party assisting the parties in finding a mutually acceptable solution.

Arbitration:

If mediation is unsuccessful or not chosen, any unresolved dispute shall be submitted to binding arbitration in accordance with the Indian Arbitration and Conciliation Act 1996. The arbitration shall be conducted by a single arbitrator mutually agreed upon by both parties or appointed by the relevant authority. The arbitration proceedings shall be confidential and held in Chennai, Tamilnadu, India, unless otherwise agreed.
