Privacy Policy And Consent For Hindo.in

Effective Date:

02.10.2023

Information We Collect:

We may collect the following types of personal information from you when you use our Platform:

  • Contact information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Demographic information (age, gender, location)
  • User account information (username, password)
  • Progress and performance data within the Platform
  • Communication history with our support team

Data Usage Purpose:

We collect and use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide access to our Hindi E-Learning Platform and its features.
  • To deliver personalized learning experiences and content based on your progress and preferences.
  • To communicate with you about updates, announcements, and support related to the Platform.
  • To improve our services, enhance user experience, and develop new features.

Data Sharing:

We will not share your personal information with third parties without your explicit consent except in the following cases:

  • When required by law or government authorities.
  • To enforce our terms of service or protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our business with appropriate safeguards.

Security Measures:

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Data Retention:

We will retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected or as required by law.

Consent:

By using our Hindi E-Learning Platform, you consent to the collection, use, and sharing of your personal information as described in this Policy. You have the right to withdraw your consent at any time by contacting us at support@hindo.in. However, please note that withdrawing consent may result in the termination of your access to the Platform.

User Rights:

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request corrections or updates to your personal data.
  • Erasure: You can request the deletion of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Objection: You can object to the processing of your personal data in certain situations.

Our Platform is not intended for children under the age of 10. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information without your consent, please contact us and we will take steps to remove the information from our systems.
